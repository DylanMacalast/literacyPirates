Who we are

Portsmouth Pirates is a new branch of the Literacy Pirates. We work with 9-12 year old children who come to us from teacher referrals. We help children in need of extra literacy support. The children, ‘pirates’, attend our learning centres, ‘ships’. Attending after school on a weekly basis for one year. Our pilot will launch in September 2020!

What we do

Teachers devise and lead our learning programme. The children read with an adult in 121 or small groups, focusing on the enjoyment of reading. Then children work on a piece of creative writing. Each term we publish their work in a unique and high quality book, film script or web app. Every term we have a big event to celebrate the publication!

The support in reading and writing comes from our family of volunteers, who we call ‘Crewmates’. Anyone can be a Crewmate! We train and support our volunteers throughout their time with us.

We are currently recruiting volunteers for the Portsmouth Pirates. Please fill our our volunteer form if you want to become a Crewmate.
