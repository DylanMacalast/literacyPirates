Ahoy there mateys!

We’re the Pompey Pirates, and we want you to join us on a journey on the high seas to magical lands of words and stories. We’re setting out on a great adventure and our ‘ship’ is a learning centre based in the city. Our ‘crew’ are teachers and volunteers. Our trainee ‘pirates’ are the children who join the ship to work with us on developing their literacy skills.

What we do

We help children get better at reading and writing (literacy). When you join our ship as a young pirate, we’ll work with you to help you enjoy reading and stories more.

Now, on a pirate ship, jobs can mean washing the decks or mending the sails – but we thought you’d like something a bit more fun! So your tasks will involve writing. This might be stories, or diaries, or film scripts. Anything that will help you use your imagination. Each term, you’ll turn your writing work into a published book, a film or even a radio show!

Our volunteers (crew) will read with you and help you explore the wonderful world of books. We are currently recruiting volunteers for the Portsmouth Pirates. Please fill out our volunteer form if you want to join our crew!
